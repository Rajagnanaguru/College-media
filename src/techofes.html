About Techofes

Techofes is an inter-college fest of Anna University-CEG. Staying true to its name, it sets up an arena for students all over the nation to come and showcase their skills. We are successfully stepping into the 73rd edition of Techofes. It is not a single event but a multitude of events, workshops and shows, primarily focussing on providing a rostrum to creativity and soulful talents. Apart from the celebra tory platform, techofes stands for environmental cause and it is celebrated based on the theme. The theme plays a pivotal role in every event and program conducted. Around 70+ enchanting events based on different arts and fest is celebrated for over 3 days. The ecstasy that accompanies the proshows like Concert Night, Choreo night and the Movie screening are inexpressible in literal sense.
