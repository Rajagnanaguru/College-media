Kurukshetra
Genre : techo management fest
Held on : End of January
Major events : K!Box, Xceed, Robowars, Godspeed, on-site and online programming contests, initiatives
Sponsor : College of Engineering, Guindy

  Kurukshetra is an international techno-management fest conducted annually by the College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG) in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It is a four-day event organized by the college's technical body, the CEG Tech Forum (CTF). The name of the fest derives from the location of the apocalyptic battle between the Kauravas and the Pandavas in the ancient Indian epic, the Mahabharata. The tag-line of the fest, "The Battle of Brains", refers to the battle. The fest was first held in 2007[1] and was the first techno-management festival in India to be awarded UNESCO patronage. Its logo, the Cyclotron, symbolize the celebration of the indomitable spirit of engineering and innovation.
